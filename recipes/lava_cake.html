Neccesitas

  1. 1/2 cup dark chocolate (about 3 ounces), chopped (or use Ghirardelli baking chips)
  2. 2 1/2 tablespoons butter
  3. 1 whole egg, room temperature
  4. 1 egg yolk, room temperature
  5. 3 tablespoons granulated sugar, (plus more for ramekins)
  6. 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  7. 1/8 teaspoon salt
  8. 2 1/2 tablespoons flour

Fundirse la mantequilla y agrega el huevo, azucar, vanilla, y sal juntos until pale color. Agrega la chocolaate fundirse y el harina. Concina para 11- 12 minuto. The middle should jiggle a bit.
